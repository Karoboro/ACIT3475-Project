Trip Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival

Arrive at the airport and check into the hotel. Spend the evening exploring the nearby attractions.

Day 2: Sightseeing

Visit famous landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral.

Day 3: Shopping and Leisure

Spend the day shopping at the famous Champs-Élysées, and relax in the evening at a local café.

Day 4: Day Trip

Take a day trip to the Palace of Versailles and explore its magnificent gardens.

Day 5: Departure

Check out of the hotel and head to the airport for the departure flight.
